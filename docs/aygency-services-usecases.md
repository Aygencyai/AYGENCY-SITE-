**Use Case 1: Hospitality Group — Operations System**

Slug: /use-cases/hospitality-operations
Sector tag: Hospitality
System type tag: Cost Reduction

Heading: MULTI-SITE RESTAURANT OPERATIONS
Subtext: "A hospitality group spending £180K+ annually on manual inventory, staff scheduling, and energy monitoring across 6 locations."

The problem:
"Six locations. Each one managing its own inventory by hand, building staff rotas on spreadsheets, and getting energy bills with no visibility into what's driving the cost. Head office gets reports weekly — by which point the waste has already happened. The data to fix all of this exists inside their POS systems, booking platforms, and utility accounts. Nobody's reading it in real time."

The system we'd build:
- Procurement Agent — monitors stock levels across all sites, auto-generates purchase orders based on booking forecasts and historical consumption, eliminates over-ordering and waste
- Energy Agent — reads smart meter data across all 6 locations, flags anomalies within hours (not months), attributes costs by site and time period, surfaces reduction opportunities
- Scheduling Agent — builds staff rotas from booking data, historical covers, and labour cost targets. Balances demand coverage with budget constraints automatically
- CEO Agent — sits above all three, identifies which locations are underperforming and why, spots patterns across sites (e.g. one location's food cost is 8% higher than the others), and surfaces strategic recommendations weekly

Estimated impact: £180K+ annual cost reduction. System live within 3 weeks.

Slug: /use-cases/professional-services-revenue
Sector tag: Professional Services
System type tag: Revenue Operations

Heading: CONSULTANCY PIPELINE AUTOMATION
Subtext: "A 40-person consultancy generating leads through manual LinkedIn outreach and referrals. Pipeline unpredictable. Growth bottlenecked by partner capacity."

- Proposal Agent — drafts scoped responses using the firm's methodology, case studies, and pricing framework. Partners review and refine rather than write from scratch
- CEO Agent — monitors pipeline health, flags when conversion rates drop, identifies which sectors and service lines are generating the most traction, and recommends where to focus

Estimated impact: 3x pipeline volume. Partners freed from 15+ hours/week of manual outreach. System live within 3 weeks.

**Use Case 4: Property Portfolio — Cost Reduction System**

Slug: /use-cases/property-cost-reduction
Sector tag: Real Estate
System type tag: Cost Reduction

Heading: COMMERCIAL PROPERTY PORTFOLIO
Subtext: "A property manager running 12 commercial buildings with manual energy monitoring, tenant communication, and maintenance scheduling."

The problem:
"Twelve buildings, each with its own utility accounts, maintenance contractors, and tenant communication channels. Energy costs are the biggest controllable expense but nobody's monitoring consumption in real time — bills arrive monthly with no granularity. Maintenance requests come in by email and phone, get triaged manually, and contractors are scheduled by one overwhelmed operations manager. Tenant queries about access, billing, and services are handled by a small team that's always behind."

The system we'd build:
- Energy Agent — monitors consumption across all 12 buildings in real time. Flags anomalies within hours — a unit left heating overnight, a system running inefficiently, usage patterns that don't match occupancy. Attributes costs by building, floor, and tenant
- Maintenance Agent — receives requests from any channel (email, portal, phone transcript), categorises by urgency and type, schedules contractors based on availability and proximity, and follows up until completion. Escalates only what needs human judgement
- Tenant Communication Agent — handles routine queries about access, billing, policies, and services. Responds within minutes, not days. Escalates complex issues with full context attached
- CEO Agent — looks across all 12 buildings to identify portfolio-wide patterns. Which buildings are most cost-efficient? Where is maintenance spend trending upward? Which tenants are at risk of churn based on complaint patterns? Surfaces strategic recommendations monthly

Estimated impact: 15-25% energy cost reduction across the portfolio. Maintenance response time from days to hours. Tenant query resolution from 48 hours to under 30 minutes. System live within 3 weeks.
