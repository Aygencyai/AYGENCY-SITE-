# Website Rebuild — Decisions Log (live)

Running record of decisions from the Q&A session. Source of truth until we convert it into the build plan. Date started: 2026-06-16.

## Posture & scope
- **Open to layout changes**, not just copy — provided the Hero and the bold/high-conviction tone survive.
- **Lead with the productized System + tiers.** The line "We don't sell packages" is retired.
- **Sacred (keep):** the Hero (headline "BUILT ONCE, COMPOUND FOREVER" + 3D block animation + the cyan typewriter line + the two buttons), and the bold high-conviction tone.
- **Not locked (can evolve):** the overall visual style/animations, and the CEO Agent section's structure (the *name* "CEO Agent" stays; the section can change).
- **Voice:** keep conviction and punch; reduce *confrontation* specifically. Confident, not accusatory.

## Brand / naming (founder overrides to the Brain — 2026-06-16)
- **Drop** "We replace workflows, not people." and "From pain point to pipeline." (founder dislikes both, despite Brain marking them "locked").
- **Hero line "Built once, compound forever"** is the loved primary line.
- **CEO Agent** is the public name (internally "Coordinator").
- **Front Desk (PA)** = a separable +1 / standalone. The other 6 specialists + CEO Agent = the operation system. Tiers are anchors (CEO+2 / +4 / up to +6–7), flexible; +Front Desk = up to 8.
- **Pricing:** show tier structure + model, **no hard numbers** (provisional, founders to confirm).
- **Proof:** labelled examples only — no named clients, no internal-build ROI. Use-cases stay but clearly marked as examples; soften past-tense "what they were dealing with".
